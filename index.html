HOT INSULATION

Hot insulation is applied to the hot surfaces of piping systems to prevent energy loss from flowing fluid. The main aim is heat conservation, and materials such as Mineral Wool, Glass Wool, and Calcium Silicate are commonly used for hot insulation. Thermal insulation, in general, reducesheat transfer, maintains comfortable indoor environments, and lowers energy costs.

The insulation is required for environment temp. exceeding 600c. The type of insulation comes under Hot Insulation. The materials required and generally applied for the Hot Insulation are as under.

Unobonded Glass wool with Handmade MAttresses Upto 400 0C. Unobonded Mineral wool with Handmade MAttresses Upto 500 0C. Lightly Resin Bonded Rockwool Mattresses with Wirenetting Upto 550 0C.

Calcium Slllicate Power / Slabs : Upto 800 0C.

Ceramic Fiber Blankets : Upto 1000 0C.
